# Homepage Gallery - Photo & Video Checklist

Each gallery category can show up to 15 photos and/or videos, displayed as a slideshow when tapped.

## Naming pattern

For each category, upload files named:
`<category-slug>-1.jpg`, `<category-slug>-2.jpg`, ... up to `<category-slug>-15.jpg`

For videos, use `.mp4` instead of `.jpg` for that number (e.g. `decoration-floral-3.mp4` instead of `decoration-floral-3.jpg`).

You don't need all 15 - upload as many as you have, starting from 1. Gaps are fine, but starting from 1 with no gaps is simplest.

## Category slugs

- 🌸 Decoration & Floral Setup -> `decoration-floral`
- 🍽️ Catering & Food -> `catering-food`
- 🎥 Photography & Videography -> `photography-videography`
- 💄 Bridal Makeup & Styling -> `bridal-makeup-styling`
- 🎉 Entertainment & Special Effects -> `entertainment-effects`
